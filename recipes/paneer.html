Paneer Curry

Paneer is the first choice for the most people to treat all the vegetarians royally

It tastes just as lovely as it looks. It's delicious both looks and taste wise.

Ingredients

  • Paneer 1kg
  • Chilli powder 250gms
  • Oil as required
  • Turmeric and Ginger paste
  • Curd 1/2 cup
  • Coriander leaves

Preparation Steps

  1. Take the Paneer into a bowl and wash it well
  2. Take a cooking pan and start heating cooking oil in it
  3. After moderate heating,add Paneer pieces to it
  4. Add chilli powder to the Paneer
  5. wait for 10 minutes and you will have your delicious Paneer curry
