chocolate-bread

Chocolate-Bread is not an easy to do recipe as it requires some experience.

but it's worth the taste to learn making a chocolate-bread. It's not advisable to start your cooking journey with this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Bread 1kg
  • Chocolate 250gms
  • Oil as required
  • Cashew and almonds
  • Cream
  • Milk

Preparation Steps

  1. Take the Bread into a bowl and wash it well
  2. Take a cooking pan and start heating cooking oil in it
  3. After moderate heating,add Bread pieces to it
  4. Add Chocolate to the Bread
  5. Apply the required cream on top of it and sugar cubes if you like
  6. That's all Ready to serve!!
